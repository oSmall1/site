CYCLING DATING - A DATE WITH ACTIVITY, BIKE AND POSITIVENESS

Cycling trips, with a touch of extreme, endurance, and overcoming oneself.
Cycling dating was born in 2023. At that time, I was preparing for my longest race of 2023, 1200km solo self-sufficient around Bosnia and Herzegovina, and so I came up with the idea that I could also show others a part of the extreme world and show what you are capable of with the right motivation, good will, and an excellent team.

In two years, we overcame obstacles with purpose and from the initial 16km uphill to an enviable 350km across Slovenia. During this time, lifelong friendships have developed, lives have changed and added value has been gained.

Being active is the most beautiful thing in life. With a company that stands by your side, guides, and encourages you, this is a unique experience. It's nice every time we get together, and I'm happy to say that we're going into 2025 with a schedule of challenges that will attract many and get the blood pumping.
